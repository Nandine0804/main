Description: An event where you can learn and unleash the limitless possibilities of doing monotonous work. RULES TO BE FOLLOWED:

  • A team can consist a minimum of 3 and a maximum of 4 members.
  • For the preliminary round, participants are requested to have at least one laptop per team.
  • For the final round, participants are requested to have laptops individually.
  • The decision made by the judges will be the final.
