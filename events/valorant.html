VALORANT

Who's Gotta Defy the Limits now?

A gaming event where you can flex your gaming skills by taking out other teams continuously until you get that throne made of Guns( Winner place ).

RULES TO BE FOLLOWED:

  • Each match has two teams of five players with 1 substitute player for each team( In case of unavailability of any players)
  • Toss decides the map in which the teams would be played.
  • No cheat codes allowed. If any team is found using cheat codes, the team would be terminated.
  • Before a match begins, each player selects a character (called an Agent) with a unique set of abilities
  • Each team can have five agents.
  • Matches have rounds, and the first team to win 13 rounds wins the match.
  • One team starts as the Attackers and the other team starts as the Defenders, and after 12 rounds, the two teams switch sides.
  • The Attackers’ goal is to plant a bomb (called the Spike) on one of several bomb sites around the map. The Defenders’ job is to, well, defend, either by defusing the Spike after it’s planted, or preventing the Attackers from planting the Spike within the round’s time limit
  • Every player in the match will get money (called Creds) that they can use during a Buy Phase to purchase weapons, armour, and abilities.
  • Matches have rounds, and the first team to win 13 rounds wins the match.
  • The amount of Creds players get is determined by their performance in the previous round.
