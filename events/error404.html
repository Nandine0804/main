ERROR 404

Branch out - push the code - stay committed

Description: Error 404 is a 2 - player debugging event, where teams must fix errors in given solutions, following specified output and algorithm. Only one team member works on the solution at a time, switching every 5 minutes with their teammate in the lobby

RULES TO BE FOLLOWED:

  • The first member of the team sits and tries his/her best to debug the errors until 5 minutes. After which the teammate switches and they start to debug from where it was left off.
  • This swap happens every 5 minutes until they have solved all the problems or the time is over.
  • Communicating with the other team member will lead to disqualification.
  • Opening a new tab or a new window or switching between applications is strictly prohibited.
  • The decision made by the judges will be the final.

YOU NEED:

ROUND 1 : 02 - 02 - 2023

Code and problem description for each of the problems will be provided. There will be questions from easy, medium and hard levels. You have to debug all the existing errors, which can be both logical and syntactic errors. Output format and algorithms are also given for your reference. For easy-level questions, algorithms will not be provided.

ROUND 2: 03 - 02 - 2023

Output format and algorithms are given for your reference. The difficulty level is raised from the preliminary level and the rest is same as the first round.
